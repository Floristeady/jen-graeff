About us

Our Story

Infinite power of giving back

Born and raised in Bellevue, Washington, Jen believes in the infinite power of giving back. This innate desire was birthed from personally experiencing the effects of the “boomerang’ throughout her life.

Whether through hosting a charity event, teaching or using her skills such as photography, the “boomerang effect” has revealed that “what you give comes back in a different form”. For this very reason, the infinity symbol is her favorite reminder of the power of generosity.

Jen obtained her Bachelor Of Arts degree in Marketing & Operations Management at Eastern Washington University.

Her professional background includes time spent at Bishop, White, Marshall & Weibel, P.S., Sabey Corporation and Slalom, acquiring expertise in market, trend, sentiment and social-marketing analysis, business forecasting and customer needs assessments.
